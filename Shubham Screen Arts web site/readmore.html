Redesigning





Redesigning is a process of redrawing and perfacting the design provided by the client.
Origanly the design created by an artis has many imperfact pattern shapes
which makes it dificult for the companies to print them on the sadies.
When this imperfect designes are used, the saides thus not look as neet and proper from
after redesigning. Redesigning is the most important process for creating designes on sarie in facteries.

Redesigning can be done using multiple colors for the amount of detail the design provided by the
costomer.The more details the designe have that much color it is going to take and will have to
use multiple layers to print them on the saries.




printing

After the redesigning process the perfected designes are prented on some large human sized
iron frames so that they can be used to print designes on saries. This iron frames
must be provided be the customer them seves our company will not provide this frames and if there is
a problem when the frame arives our company is not responsible for that issue.

The designes are printed on this frames with the help of a very big printer. This printer are
created for the sole perpose of printing designes on this frames and cannot
be used for other types of printing purposes.This printer are avalable in the market at a very high
prise and are very complecated to use.
